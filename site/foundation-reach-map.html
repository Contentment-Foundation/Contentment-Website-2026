How to use this map

  1. Pan — click and drag the map with your mouse
  2. Zoom — mouse scroll wheel (up = in, down = out), pinch on trackpad, or the + / − buttons
  3. Filter — pick a region with the chips above
  4. Preview — hover a green country to see its name
  5. Read — click a pinned story card; hover a two-card stack to spread them
